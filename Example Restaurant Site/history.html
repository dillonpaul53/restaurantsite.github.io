The Friendly Antler was established in 2016 with one mission: To serve great food to the residents and visitors of Estes Park, Colorado. While we are new to the restaurant business in town, our management and employees bring together years of experience in the culinary industry. Our menu has always been reflective of our two main inspirations homestyle and southwestern cuisine. As a recent Best New Restaurant recipient from the Estes Gazette we have worked hard to establish a name for ourselves over this short period of time, but our efforts to impress are never complete!
