We take great pleasure in introducing our head chef Jeffrey Saunders. Chef Jeffrey has been gracing the resteraunt industry with his incredible talents for the past twenty years. Around the time The Friendly Antler opened we offered the head chef positon to Jeffrey. He stepped down from his work with a five-star resteraunt to come back to his homwe town and share his expertise with old pals and new friends alike.

Jeffrey's flair for soutwest flavors and home town inspired cuisine makes him the perfect person design are menu of delectable meals. His knowledge and experience in the culinary field are a large part of why The Friendly Antler experience is so exquisite. Offering the best service starts with offering the best food. We have Jeffrey to thank for that!
